Privacy Policy

At AskSurgeons, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and mobile application.

How We Use Your Information

- To connect you with qualified surgeons and provide consultation support.
- To improve our website, mobile app, and services.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Information is shared only with relevant healthcare professionals if required for consultation purposes, with your consent.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no online platform can be 100% secure, and we encourage you to use caution when sharing sensitive medical details.
